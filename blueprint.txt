AI-Powered JEE Test Generation SaaS
Complete Product Blueprint & Development
 Prompt Guide
This document contains the complete product architecture, execution roadmap, phase-wise development prompts,
SaaS planning, technical structure, AI workflows, and monetization strategy for building a scalable AI-powered JEE
examination platform. Core modules include: - Question Paper Generator - Student CBT System - Institute SaaS
Dashboard - AI Question Extraction Engine - Analytics Engine - Doubt Solving Marketplace - PDF Generation System
- Scalable Backend Infrastructure

Final Strategic Advice
The biggest strength of this business is not the frontend UI — it is the structured question database, dynamic test
generation engine, scalable architecture, and automation pipeline. Focus first on: 1. Structured question management
2. Dynamic paper generation 3. Reliable CBT infrastructure Avoid overbuilding in the beginning. Start with
institute-focused SaaS validation. Scale slowly into AI extraction and doubt marketplace systems. This idea has
strong startup potential because it combines: - SaaS - AI - Marketplace - Education Infrastructure - Analytics -
Scalable recurring revenue
